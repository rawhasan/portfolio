Projects I've Developed

GeoRail Bangladesh

A web app to display Bangladesh Railway information on the map.

Live Site

Features of this project

Train Route

Display route of every train in Bangladesh on the map, including details of the trains e.g., carriage type, weekly off days, stop time at the stations on the way, available classes on the trains including details of each class, and available food on the train with their price.

Station Schedule

The full schedule of every train station in Bangladesh. Trains are grouped by their destinations and sorted by departure time. Includes train numbers and their weekly off days. The location of the stations is also displayed on the map to provide an excellent geographical idea.

Transit Suggestion

Display a list of all the trains between a given origin and destination station. It also shows the return trains. Trains are sorted by departure times and include weekly off days. Both of the stations are also located on the map to provide an excellent geographical idea.
